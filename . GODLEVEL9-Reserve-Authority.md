# GODLEVEL9 Reserve Authority

## Definition
The **GODLEVEL9 Reserve Authority** is my central framework for organizing creation, ownership, and long-term value. It acts as a control layer that binds my outputs into one system.

## What it integrates
- **Creative output** (music, writing, visuals, games)
- **Intellectual property** (titles, characters, worlds, symbols)
- **Digital assets** (where applicable)
- **Long-term value systems** (archiving, editions, catalogs)

## Architecture (4 layers)
### 1) Creative Layer
Where works are created: albums, scripts, drafts, releases.

### 2) IP Layer
Where works become owned assets: titles, series, brands, universe rules.

### 3) Digital Layer
Where systems can be tracked or distributed: metadata, versioning, token-linked utilities (if used).

### 4) Vault Layer
Where everything is preserved: edition control, archives, catalogs, “no-loss” storage logic.

## Operating principles
- **Ownership first**
- **Repeatable structure**
- **Scalable catalog**
- **One ecosystem, not fragments**
- **Documentation over noise**

## Canon governance
When I update a system definition, I log:
- What changed
- Why it changed
- What it affects (pages, projects, terms)
